# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

As a college student, maintaining responsible behavior on social media is important for your personal and professional growth. This checklist highlights key Do’s and Don’ts to follow.

---

## ✅ Do’s

- Maintain a **professional and respectful tone** in your posts and comments  
- Regularly review and update your **privacy settings**  
- Share only **accurate and verified information**  
- Build a **positive digital footprint** by showcasing achievements and skills  
- Think before posting and consider its impact on your **future opportunities**  

---

## ❌ Don’ts

- Do not share **personal or sensitive information** (e.g., address, passwords)  
- Avoid posting **offensive or inappropriate content**  
- Do not engage in **online arguments or trolling**  
- Avoid spreading **fake news or unverified information**  
- Do not overshare content that can harm your **professional image**  

---

## 📌 Conclusion

Responsible use of social media helps in building a strong online presence and protects your reputation. Always be mindful of what you post and how you interact online.
