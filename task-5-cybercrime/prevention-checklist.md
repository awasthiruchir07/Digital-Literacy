#  Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

Staying safe online is essential for protecting your personal, academic, and financial information. Follow these actionable tips to reduce the risk of cyber threats.

---

##  Prevention Tips

- Use **strong, unique passwords** for every account  
- Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** wherever possible  
- Do not click on **unknown or suspicious links**  
- Always check for **https://** before entering sensitive information  
- Avoid using **public Wi-Fi** for banking or important logins  
- Keep your device updated with **security patches and antivirus**  
- Limit sharing of **personal information** on social media  
- Regularly review your **privacy settings**  

---

##  Financial / UPI Safety

- Never share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details** with anyone  
- Double-check the **UPI ID or recipient name** before sending money  
- Do not approve **unknown collect/payment requests**  
- Avoid scanning **random or suspicious QR codes**  

---

## Reporting Cyber Crime

If you become a victim of cyber fraud, act immediately:

-  Report at: **https://cybercrime.gov.in**  
-  Helpline: **1930**

---

##  Important Note

Quick reporting increases the chances of recovering lost money and preventing further damage. Always stay alert and think before you click.
