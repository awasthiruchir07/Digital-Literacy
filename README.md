## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on developing digital literacy skills essential for students in today’s digital world. It covers key areas such as understanding digital tools, maintaining online safety, building a professional presence, and practicing effective digital communication.

The project includes practical tasks like creating an infographic, designing a quiz, writing professional emails, and building awareness about responsible social media use.

---

## 📚 Module Summaries

### 1. Digital Literacy Basics
Understanding what digital literacy means and why it is important for students in academics and professional life.

### 2. Digital Tools for Students
Exploration of useful platforms like GitHub for coding, Canva for design, and Google Forms for surveys and quizzes.

### 3. Safe Internet Practices
Learning how to stay safe online, including password security, phishing awareness, and UPI safety.

### 4. Professional Online Presence
Building a positive digital identity using platforms like LinkedIn and maintaining professionalism online.

### 5. Digital Communication & Email Etiquette
Practicing formal communication through emails and understanding how tone and clarity impact communication.
